Why do you like Lille?

Lille is a very nice and friendly city, neither too big nor too small. You can't be bored, there is always something to do.

Your favorite spots in Lille?

The "Vieux Lille" (old city) with its many beautiful streets. There are lot of bars very cosy and cool. An other spot very nice to walk is "La Citadelle", a very peacefull park.

Why do you want to become a Peetayer?

To help and meet new people. Share my experience and local tips in Lille. I know what it's like to arrive in a new city and I enjoyed being helped by locals and expatriates. My turn to do the same.

What are your interests?

I really like going out with my friends for a drink. But also different and cultural outings like going to the theater, go to concerts, museum...

What about Florine?

Florine studied in Lille, and after some years in Paris and abroad, she came back in the "Vieux Lille" (old town). Young entrepreneur, she likes meet new people, in particular strangers to share with them the local culture, speak english and spanish, and to discover the beautiful city of Lille.
