Feel confident studying abroad, our locals will take care of you !

Peetaya is a social network that allows students to share an experience with a local or an enthusiast of his city during his stay abroad (internship, university exchange, roadtrip, placement year, etc.)

How it works?

1. I SEND

A request to Peetaya to be put in contact with a Peetayer for my arrival in my future city or during my trip

2. I RECEIVE

One or more Peetayers profiles so that I can choose the one who suits me the most (ultra-personalized)

3. I BOOK

My Peetayer by confirming the payment. Then I receive his personal details to contact him
